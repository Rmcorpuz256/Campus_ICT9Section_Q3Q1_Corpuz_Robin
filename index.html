A schoolgirl's mysterious paper finds its way into the hands of Caleb Koestler, the son of professor John Koestler (Nicolas Cage), fifty years after it was buried in a time capsule. John deciphers the encoded message, which correctly enumerates every significant calamity of the last 50 years and foretells three more tragedies, one of which will be a worldwide cataclysm. John seeks the assistance of the prophetic author's daughter and granddaughter in an attempt to prevent the ultimate catastrophe when his warnings are ignored.
